7-Day Itinerary

1

Athens Marina → Aegina

17 nm · ~3h sailing · Easy

Depart from Alimos Marina and sail south to the charming island of Aegina. Explore the ancient Temple of Aphaia and enjoy fresh pistachios at the harbour tavernas.

2

Aegina → Agistri

6 nm · ~1h sailing · Easy

A short hop to tiny Agistri — perfect for snorkelling in the turquoise waters of Aponisos beach. Pine forests meet the sea here.

3

Agistri → Poros

15 nm · ~2.5h sailing · Easy

Sail to Poros and anchor in the scenic strait between the island and the Peloponnese. The clock tower hike offers stunning sunset views.

4

Poros → Hydra

12 nm · ~2h sailing · Easy

The jewel of the Saronic — Hydra is car-free with stone mansions cascading down to the harbour. Snorkel at Bisti Bay on the south coast.

5

Hydra → Spetses

11 nm · ~2h sailing · Easy

Sail to the elegant island of Spetses. Rent bikes to explore, swim at Zogeria beach, and dine at the Old Harbour as the sun sets.

6

Spetses → Aegina via Dokos

25 nm · ~4h sailing · Moderate

The longest leg — stop at uninhabited Dokos for a swim in Skintos Bay (one of the best snorkelling spots in the Gulf), then continue to Aegina.

7

Aegina → Athens Marina

17 nm · ~3h sailing · Easy

A relaxed final sail back to Athens. Arrive by early afternoon to allow time for provisioning return and an evening in the city.
