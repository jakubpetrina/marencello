7-Day Itinerary

1
Athens Marina → Aegina
17 nm · ~3h sailing · Easy
Board & provisions. Afternoon sail to Aegina. Swim at Moni Island (great snorkelling!).
2
Aegina → Agistri
6 nm · ~1h sailing · Easy
Short hop to the hidden gem of Agistri. Crystal-clear Aponisos beach for snorkelling.
3
Agistri → Poros
15 nm · ~2.5h sailing · Easy
Sail to charming Poros Town. Evening taverna dinner along the waterfront.
4
Poros → Hydra
12 nm · ~2h sailing · Easy
Car-free Hydra island. Anchor at Bisti Bay for the best snorkelling of the trip.
5
Hydra → Spetses
11 nm · ~2h sailing · Easy
Pine-covered Spetses. Visit Agioi Anargyroi beach, explore the old harbour town.
6
Spetses → Aegina (via Dokos)
25 nm · ~4h sailing · Moderate
Longest day. Stop at uninhabited Dokos for a wild swim, then overnight at Aegina.
7
Aegina → Athens Marina
17 nm · ~3h sailing · Easy
Morning swim, leisurely sail back to Athens. Check-out by 09:00 Saturday.
