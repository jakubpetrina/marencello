About This Yacht

The Lagoon 46 "Sea Breeze" is a stunning 2023 catamaran based in ACI Marina Split. With her spacious 4-cabin layout, she comfortably accommodates up to 10 guests in style. The flybridge offers a panoramic helm station and generous lounging area, while the cockpit features a large dining table perfect for al fresco meals.

Fully equipped with air conditioning, watermaker, electric winches, and a comprehensive electronics package. Water toys include a stand-up paddleboard, snorkelling gear, and a tender with outboard engine.
