About This Yacht

The Lagoon 46 "Sea Breeze" is a modern, spacious catamaran designed for the ultimate charter experience. With her sleek lines and generous living spaces, she offers an unparalleled combination of performance and comfort on the Adriatic Sea.

Her open-plan saloon features panoramic windows that flood the interior with natural light, while the expansive flybridge provides the perfect vantage point for watching sunsets over the Croatian islands. Each of the four cabins has its own ensuite bathroom, ensuring privacy and comfort for all guests.

Whether you're an experienced sailor looking for a bareboat adventure or prefer the luxury of a skippered charter, Sea Breeze adapts perfectly to your style of cruising. Her shallow draft allows access to secluded bays and anchorages that larger yachts simply cannot reach.
