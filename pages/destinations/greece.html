About Greece Sailing

Greece is the ultimate sailing destination, blessed with over 6,000 islands and islets scattered across the Aegean and Ionian seas. The Meltemi winds of summer provide exhilarating sailing conditions, while the sheltered Saronic Gulf and the gentle Ionian coast welcome beginners with calm, predictable breezes. Every harbour tells a story stretching back millennia, from the temples of Delos to the windmills of Mykonos.

Greek sailing is as much about the culture as the navigation. Anchor in a secluded cove for a swim in impossibly blue water, then step ashore for grilled octopus at a seaside taverna. The variety is staggering: volcanic calderas in Santorini, lush green hillsides in Corfu, and the wild Cycladic landscapes shaped by wind and sea. With a mature charter infrastructure and warm hospitality at every port, Greece remains the gold standard of Mediterranean sailing.

Sailing Regions

From the windswept Cyclades to the sheltered Ionian, each region offers a distinct Greek experience.

Cyclades

The iconic Greek islands: Mykonos, Santorini, Paros, and Naxos. Whitewashed villages perched on volcanic cliffs above deep blue waters, with spirited Meltemi winds for experienced sailors.

Saronic Gulf

The gateway to Greek sailing, just a short hop from Athens. Explore the car-free island of Hydra, the pine forests of Poros, and the ancient theatre of Epidaurus, all in sheltered waters.

Ionian Islands

Lush, green, and sheltered from the Meltemi. Corfu, Lefkada, Kefalonia, and Zakynthos offer gentle sailing, emerald waters, and Venetian-influenced architecture.

Best Time to Sail

Greece rewards sailors from late spring through early autumn.

local_florist

Spring (May - Jun)

Pleasant 22-26°C temperatures with moderate winds. Islands are green, tourist crowds have not yet arrived, and charter prices are favourable.

wb_sunny

Summer (Jul - Aug)

Peak season with 28-32°C heat and strong Meltemi winds in the Aegean. Vibrant nightlife, bustling ports, and ideal conditions for thrill-seeking sailors.

eco

Autumn (Sep - Oct)

Warm seas at 24-27°C, calmer winds, and golden sunsets. A local favourite, with the grape harvest season adding depth to the culinary experience.
